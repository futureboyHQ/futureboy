The Dream Team

How We Came To Be

It all started 2 years ago when we met to startup a dental software company. Sacha was the talented designer, Philippe the shaolin coder, and Gregory the visionary. After moving in together and failing to materialize that first project, we dug deeper than ever before into our internal abysses. After 8 months of intangible progress, we are at it again with a new shared belief system, an incredible friendship, and a solid foundation. Chloe, who's been going at it alone meanwhile, adds another dimension to the team with her elegance, maturity, and stillness. We. are. one.
