The Dream Team

How We Came To Be

Two years ago, we had an objective: End the wall of business ignorance in the dental industry by helping doctors to truly understand their practices. Sacha was the talented designer, Philippe the shaolin coder, and Gregory the visionary. After moving in together and failing to materialize that first project, we dug deeper than ever before into our internal abysses. After 8 months of intangible progress, we are back! Our toolkit: a new shared belief system, an incredible friendship, and the drive to move mountains. Meanwhile, Chloé who's been going at it alone, adds another dimension to the team with her elegance, maturity, and stillness. We. are. one.
