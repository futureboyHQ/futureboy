Vision

It Starts with an Intention

The intention to inspire you to live with purpose & tame your fears. Our dream is to see a world in which everyone connects with the essence of being through flow, experimentations, and love. As we were working hard to evolve and reconsider everything, one question emerged: What if we opened our doors and shared our quest to learn to live with purpose?

Without hesitation, it became clear that we had to dedicate ourselves to the task. We sincerely hope that our journey will inspire you to follow your heart & that you'll evolve as much as we do. In the end, we promise you an authentic and sincere environment as we strive to live up to the principles of Maat: Truth, Justice, and Harmony.

Our Quest

Tune In & Take Action

In the last 5 years, we've oriented everything towards the process of self-discovery. It started with Gregory experimenting with visualization and realizing that we hold much more power than we think. From there, it's been a crazy rollercoaster, but a really great one. In fact, it feels like we've been fast tracked to free ourselves from a belief system that brought all of us down, and now, we're ready to share that process back with you!

Way of Life

Universal Principles

In our quest for self-discovery we have collected teachings from people living their dream. Visionary leaders, masters, high-performers & enlightened sages have been met along our journey. These teachings come from life experiences, looking within, ancient civilizations, philosophy & sacred texts.

Our hope is that these guiding principles & concepts will uplift your life & those around you by orders of magnitude. As they have for us. Below, you'll find the list of concepts we've discovered so far and try our best to live by:
